Speaking and Listening Development (Birth–5 Years)

Birth–1 Year

  • Responds to sounds and voices
  • Makes cooing and babbling sounds
  • Uses eye contact and gestures

1–2 Years

  • Says first words
  • Follows simple instructions
  • Uses gestures along with words

2–3 Years

  • Uses short sentences
  • Asks simple questions
  • Enjoys songs and stories

3–5 Years

  • Uses longer sentences
  • Shares ideas and experiences
  • Listens to stories and conversations

Supporting children’s speaking and listening skills during early childhood helps build strong foundations for later learning and literacy.
